Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 20, 2026

Overview

URL Locale Cleaner ("the Extension") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how the Extension operates and what data it handles.

Data Collection and Processing

The Extension does not collect, store, record, or transmit any personal data. All URL processing is performed locally within your browser using our client-side sanitization logic.

How It Works

  • Local Processing Only: When you click the toolbar icon or use the context menu, the current tab's URL is read from your browser session and cleaned locally on your device.
  • Clipboard Access: The cleaned URL is written directly to your clipboard. This is a browser-level operation and no data is sent to external servers.
  • No External Requests: The Extension does not make any network requests, API calls, or contact any remote services.
  • No Storage: URLs, clipboard data, or any processing history is never stored on disk or in the cloud.

Permissions Explanation

The Extension requests the following permissions, all used exclusively for local functionality:

  • clipboardWrite – To place the cleaned URL on your clipboard.
  • tabs – To read the current tab's URL for cleaning.
  • contextMenus – To add a "Copy Clean URL" option to the right-click menu.
  • scripting – To execute the clipboard write operation within the page context.
  • activeTab – To access the currently active tab.

Third-Party Services

The Extension does not use third-party analytics, tracking services, or advertising networks. It does not integrate with or send data to any external service.

User Control

You can uninstall the Extension at any time from your browser's extension management page. Uninstalling immediately stops all functionality and access to your browser data.

Changes to This Policy

If we update this Privacy Policy, we will post the changes here with an updated "Last Updated" date. Your continued use of the Extension constitutes acceptance of any changes.
